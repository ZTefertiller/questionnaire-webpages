Participant Information

In this study, you will be filling out a questionnaire about personality traits and perceptual experiences.

The questionnaire should take about 10 minutes.

Why is this study being conducted?

Every day we make decisions: how to get to work, what to eat, what to wear, etc. We want to see how people understand risk in the decisions they make.


Is there a personal benefit from participating in the study?

You will not gain any personal benefit from participating. The results of the study aim to advance our understanding of important cognitive processes.


Are there risks associated with participating in the study?

Since this study is conducted online, the risks are minimal. However, computer-based tasks can be surprisingly tiring. Take this moment to grab a drink of water or quickly use the restroom. We ask that you take your time with the questionnaires and do not stop once it is started. A full minute of inactivity in a questionnaire will result in removal from the study.


Will there be any additional costs?

There are no costs for you to participate in the study.


Will I receive compensation for my time?

You will receive €6.00 per hour. Upon completing the entire experiment, you will receive a total of €2.00 (paid through Prolific).

What is expected of me?

We expect you to complete the questionnaire seated upright at a table or desk in a distraction-free environment (browser window maximized, sound on, and no music, tabs, or other applications in the background). Please silence your phone and place it out of sight for the entire duration. It is essential to answer the questions conscientiously.


Can I withdraw from the study later?

Your participation is voluntary, and you can withdraw from the study at any time without providing a reason and without any penalties. Once the data has been fully anonymized (excluding your pseudonymized Prolific ID), it will no longer be traceable to you, and deletion will no longer be possible from that point.
