Belief and Experience Questionnaire

Please answer all questions honestly and rate the scales according to your own experiences. To see instructions again click here.

Instructions

This questionnaire asks questions about sensations and perceptions you may have experienced. Some of the experiences are unusual, some of them are more everyday.

We are NOT interested in experiences under the influence of drugs/alcohol.

IT IS IMPORTANT THAT YOU ANSWER ALL QUESTIONS.

For the questions you answer YES to, we are interested in:
(a) how distressing you found the experience
(b) how distracting you found it; and
(c) how often the experience occurs.

Please select the number which corresponds most closely to how distressing this belief is, how often you think about it, and how much you believe that it is true. If you answer NO please move on to the next question.
