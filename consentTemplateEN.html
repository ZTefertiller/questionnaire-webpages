Hello and welcome to the experiment!

You will be presented with a series of questionnaires and then two behavioral tasks. For example, we will have you estimate numbers of objects on the screen. You'll be given more detailed instructions prior to each task. The questionnaires should take 45 minutes or less in total, so please try to answer questions honestly and quickly (if in doubt, follow your gut and move on!). The tasks should take a total of 30 minutes.

It is important that when you begin the tasks that you complete them start-to-finish in one sitting and not take any breaks.

Before proceeding, if necessary please take this moment to stretch, use the restroom, or grab a glass of water.
